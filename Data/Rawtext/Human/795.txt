Who Spent Their Last Stimulus Checks?

As President Biden works with Congress to pass the American Rescue Plan, policymakers are debating who should receive the next round of stimulus checks. The essence of the debate boils down to simple questions: Which Americans need help right now? Will they spend the money they get at struggling businesses, which might then bring their employees back to work and get our economy going again?
