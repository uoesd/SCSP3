As the situation worsens, the international response has begun to take shape. The United States and the United Kingdom have announced plans to deploy troops to secure Kabul's airport, aiming to facilitate the evacuation of their citizens and eligible Afghans. The chaos at the airports, however, tells its own story. Despite many holding tickets, countless individuals have been unable to board their flights. Scenes of panic and despair are rampant as people grapple with the possibility of being left behind.
