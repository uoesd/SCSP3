Harry and Meghan Honor Martin Luther King Day by Supporting Volunteers with Lunch

In a heartfelt tribute to Martin Luther King Jr., Prince Harry and Meghan Markle made a notable gesture of gratitude and solidarity by offering lunch to volunteers of The Mission Continues, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering military veterans to continue their service in communities across the United States. The couple, who have slowly carved a niche for themselves in philanthropy since stepping back from royal duties, took the opportunity to acknowledge Dr. King's enduring legacy through their act of kindness.

Expressing their appreciation, Harry and Meghan shared a message highlighting the significance of Dr. King's legacy and the ongoing need for community service. "Today, as we honor the life and legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., we are reminded of the importance of serving our communities," they said. "Let's all strive to live by Dr. King's enduring principles of love and service to others."

This is not the first time that Harry and Meghan have rolled up their sleeves to help The Mission Continues. In November, they were seen distributing food with the organization, demonstrating their commitment to community service.
