According to Tuberville, Trump's response was to tell him to continue objecting to the election results, indicating a disregard for the unfolding violence and the safety of individuals at the Capitol.

The evidence presented by the House impeachment managers paints a damning picture of Trump's role in the events of January 6. As the trial continues, it remains to be seen how this will impact the former president and the broader implications for American politics.
