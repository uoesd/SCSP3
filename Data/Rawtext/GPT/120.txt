Alicia's eventual decision to get vaccinated was influenced by her battle with asthma and the advisories of her doctor. Her vaccination protected her from severe symptoms and possibly saved her life, showcasing the vaccine's effectiveness amidst a cloud of skepticism and misinformation.

Mississippi's struggle against COVID-19 mirrors wider challenges faced by the United States as the Delta variant continues to spread, prompting revised mask mandates and safety protocols even for the vaccinated population. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has highlighted that while breakthrough infections among vaccinated individuals can occur with the Delta variant, the symptoms are usually milder compared to those in unvaccinated people.
