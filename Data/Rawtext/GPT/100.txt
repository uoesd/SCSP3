Aquila's Nest Vineyards Celebrates Anniversary with Unique Outdoor Events

Newtown, CT - Aquila's Nest Vineyards, a popular winery in Newtown, Connecticut, is thrilled to announce a series of new events to celebrate its first anniversary. The winery has quickly become a favorite destination for both locals and visitors to the Greater Danbury area, known for its picturesque setting and innovative entertainment options. As part of the anniversary celebrations, Aquila's Nest is introducing a unique event that combines fun, education, and wineâ€” the 'Halo-Wine Scavenger Hunt.'

Set to take place on October 30th at the vineyard's scenic Aquila's Pumpkin yard, the scavenger hunt requires participants to reserve their spots in advance and pay a nominal entry fee of $3 per person. This engaging event invites participants to explore the vineyard, following clues that will educate them about the winemaking process and the story behind Aquila's Nest Vineyards. It's not just an adventure but a chance to immerse in the world of wine in the most interactive way.

Co-founder Neviana Zhgaba expressed her excitement about the scavenger hunt, saying, "We wanted to create a fun outdoor activity that promotes social distancing while allowing our guests to learn about wine and explore the beauty of Aquila's Nest. We believe it's a great way to celebrate our first anniversary, offering an experience that's both safe and enjoyable."

Participants who successfully complete the scavenger hunt are rewarded with a free glass of wine, adhering to the age restriction of 21 or over to participate. This initiative not only aims to entertain but also to educate adults about the art of wine in a novel and engaging manner.

The vineyard's press release and testimonials from previous guests share personal positive experiences, highlighting the vineyard's atmospheric charm and the variety of family-friendly options available. Aquila's Nest has become synonymous with great times and good wine, offering a unique blend of entertainment and relaxation.

Beyond the scavenger hunt, Aquila's Nest Vineyards has a lineup of upcoming themed events like chocolate & wine pairing sessions and a 'Hallowine Sunset Art Walk.' These events are part of the vineyard's mission to provide diverse entertainment options during a period that requires social distancing, with most events featuring live music and food trucks to enhance the experience.

Neviana Zhgaba believes that these events showcase Aquila's Nest Vineyards as a notable destination in the area, saying, "Our goal is to offer a safe haven where people can relax, enjoy good wine, and partake in unique activities. We're excited to be a part of the vibrant Danbury area, which really glows at night, reflecting the lively local atmosphere."

Aquila's Nest Vineyards invites everyone to participate in their anniversary events, promising a blend of fun, learning, and exceptional wine. Whether you're a local or a visitor, Aquila's Nest Vineyards is a must-visit destination in the Greater Danbury area, offering something special for everyone.
