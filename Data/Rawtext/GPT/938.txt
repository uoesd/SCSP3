"My vote was compelled by the oath I swore to the Constitution," she stated, adding weight to the argument for adhering to constitutional responsibilities over party politics.

"If we continue down this path, we're going to turn off a generation of voters," Meijer observed. His stance has resulted in censure from GOP county groups and a primary challenge from Tom Norton, indicating the deep divisions within the party over its direction and the loyalty demanded toward Trump.
