Santorum's departure comes in the wake of his controversial comments regarding Native Americans, which ignited a substantial outcry from both the public and various advocacy groups.

In April, during an address to a conservative youth organization, Santorum asserted that the United States "birthed from nothing" and significantly downplayed the cultural contributions of Native American communities to American society. These remarks were met with immediate criticism, leading to widespread demands for his dismissal from CNN, where he was frequently featured.

The Native American Journalists Association was among the first to express disdain, calling Santorum's comments dangerously misleading and a disservice to the historical truth. They, along with others, accused Santorum of erasing the vast and rich cultural heritage of Native Americans in shaping the nation.

Facing mounting pressure, Santorum appeared on "Cuomo Prime Time" in an attempt to clarify his statements. He claimed to have misspoken, acknowledging the mistreatment of Native American peoples throughout U.S. history. However, critics noted that his on-air explanations fell short of a sincere apology for the initial comments.

CNN's decision to sever ties with Santorum reportedly stemmed from an internal sense of dissatisfaction with his attempts to defend his controversial statements. While Santorum was provided a platform to explain his remarks, it became apparent that his appearance did little to quell the uproar or mend the breach of trust with the audience.

A spokesperson for CNN confirmed Santorum's departure from the network but declined to elaborate on the specifics of the decision-making process. This silence leaves room for speculation about the various factors that may have influenced the network's move to part ways with the former senator.

Following his controversial comments and the subsequent defense, Santorum found himself increasingly sidelined at CNN. Several sources within the network noted that he had been effectively "benched," as none of the anchors were willing to book him for their segments, signaling a loss of internal support.

This incident marks not the first time a high-profile commentator has exited CNN under a cloud of controversy. Figures like Jeffery Lord and Paris Dennard have previously left the network amid their own contentious circumstances.

While at CNN, Santorum was an outspoken advocate for elements of the Trump agenda, frequently contributing a conservative perspective to the network's political discussions. His departure represents a notable loss of one of the few prominent conservative voices on the channel.

As of this report, Santorum has not publicly responded to inquiries about his departure from CNN. His silence leaves unanswered questions about his perspective on the network's decision and his future plans within the realm of political commentary.

Rick Santorum's exit from CNN underscores the complexities and challenges media outlets face in balancing a spectrum of political viewpoints while maintaining a commitment to historical accuracy and sensitivity to diverse cultural contributions.
