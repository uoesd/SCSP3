This pattern of hypocrisy not only tarnishes our national character but poses a grave risk to how our children perceive values. When adults in positions of power fail to embody the virtues of honesty, respect, and fairness, we are teaching our youth that these principles are negotiable. It's a lesson that could have far-reaching consequences, eroding the very foundation of our democratic society and undermining our efforts to nurture a generation capable of empathy, integrity, and true leadership.
