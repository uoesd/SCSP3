This move by Pence is not only an acknowledgment of the incoming administration but also an affirmation of the longstanding tradition of comity and mutual respect between outgoing and incoming leaders, a tradition President Trump has largely ignored.

Furthermore, Pence has continued to fulfill his ceremonial duties in the final days of his vice presidency. He has made efforts to express his gratitude to the National Guard members stationed around the Capitol, acknowledging their role in protecting the integrity of the inauguration ceremony against potential disruptions.

The interaction between Pence and Harris represents a crucial return to the norms of political civility and bipartisanship, especially at a time when the nation is deeply divided. The call is a beacon of hope for many who value the peaceful and respectful transfer of power as a cornerstone of American democracy.
