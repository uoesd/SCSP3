According to the user, who goes by the name @NinaCelie, the altercation took place at Merry Piglett's Mexican Grill in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, on Aug. 27, 2021. Celie recounts how her boyfriend, referred to as Matt, possesses an 'insane photographic memory,' which aided in recalling the specifics of the encounter between Petito and Laundrie.

The claim holds particular significance as it closely aligns with the timeline compiled by ABC News, which notes the last communication between Petito and her mother occurring on Aug. 25. Moreover, the incident offers potentially new insights into the dynamics between Petito and Laundrie in the days leading up to Petito's disappearance.

During the reported incident, Petito is described as being in a state of hysteria, crying uncontrollably, while Laundrie was allegedly involved in a dispute with the restaurant's hostess. Celie details that Laundrie's interaction with the staff escalated to the point of speaking with the restaurant's manager multiple times.

Celie admits that the significance of the encounter did not resonate with her and her boyfriend until he had a sudden recollection of the incident on a Wednesday morning, prompting her to come forward with the information.

She further described Laundrie's demeanor towards the hostess as 'violent' and 'relentless,' shedding light on the intensity of the conflict and raising questions about Laundrie's behavior.

In response to these developments, the FBI has issued a call for anyone who was in the area from Aug. 27 to Aug. 30, 2021, to come forward with any potential evidence, reflecting the importance of gathering all possible information related to the case.

This revelation comes amidst the backdrop of mounting public and investigative focus on the timeline leading up to Petito's disappearance. Laundrie returned to his home on Sept. 1, driving Petito's van, but without Petito by his side.

The Petito family reported Gabby missing on Sept. 11, launching a nationwide search that captured the attention of the public and media alike. Laundrie was subsequently reported missing by his family on Sept. 17.

Tragically, on Sept. 19, search teams discovered Petito's body in the Bridger-Teton National Park, with her identity being confirmed two days later on Sept. 21.

The new eyewitness account stands as a potentially pivotal piece of the puzzle in understanding the tragic events leading up to the loss of Gabby Petito and may assist in piecing together the final days of her life.
