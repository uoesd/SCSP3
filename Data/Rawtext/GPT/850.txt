This encounter came at a significant time -- it was the week of the Masters, a tournament both men had dearly hoped to play in this year but couldn't due to different setbacks.

Fowler, who missed qualifying for the Masters for the first time in ten years, found himself watching the iconic event alongside Woods, a five-time champion at Augusta National. The visit marked Fowler's first time seeing Woods since his devastating car crash in February, a scenario that has captivated the golf world and fans globally.

Woods, whose triumphs at Augusta are legendary, and Fowler, with his impressive record of five top-12 finishes at the Masters, found solace in each other's company.
