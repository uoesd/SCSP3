Tom Brady Launches Fashion Label 'Brady': Aims to Rival Jordan Brand

Tom Brady, the legendary NFL quarterback, is expanding his empire with the launch of his own fashion label, 'Brady'. The brand, which will debut in December, will feature activewear and training clothes, and promises to be a game-changer in the sportswear industry.

Brady has collaborated with Jens Grede, co-founder of Skims and Good American, and Dao-Yi Chow, co-founder of Public School New York, to create a brand that will compete with the likes of Michael Jordan's Jordan Brand. Grede will serve as the brand's co-founder and CEO, bringing his expertise in building successful direct-to-consumer fashion brands.

"We're thrilled to announce the launch of Brady, a brand that will revolutionize the sportswear industry," said Jens Grede, CEO of Brady. "Our goal is to create the finest sports brand in the world, and with Tom's passion and dedication, we're confident we can achieve that."

The 'Brady' brand has been in development for three years, and Grede's involvement hints at a highly anticipated launch and potential future success. Grede's history of building successful brands, including Skims and Good American, which are expected to reach a combined revenue of $500 million in 2021, speaks to his ability to create a successful brand.

"I'm excited to partner with Jens and Dao-Yi on this new venture," said Tom Brady. "I've always been passionate about fashion, and I'm looking forward to bringing my vision to the sportswear industry."

Dao-Yi Chow, co-founder of Public School New York, also expressed his excitement about the new brand. "I'm thrilled to be a part of this project," he said. "Tom's commitment to excellence is inspiring, and I'm confident that 'Brady' will be a huge success."

The 'Brady' brand will offer a range of activewear and training clothes, designed to meet the needs of athletes and fitness enthusiasts alike. The brand's mission is to provide high-quality, functional clothing that is both comfortable and stylish.

"We want to create a brand that stands for excellence, innovation, and style," said Grede. "We believe that 'Brady' will be a game-changer in the sportswear industry, and we're excited to share it with the world."

With the launch of 'Brady', Tom Brady is expanding his empire beyond the football field. The brand's debut in December is highly anticipated, and with the expertise of Grede and Chow, 'Brady' is poised to become one of the largest namesake sportswear brands in the world.
