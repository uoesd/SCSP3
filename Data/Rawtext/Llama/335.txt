Ever Given Ship Stuck in Suez Canal Gets Immortalized in Microsoft Flight Simulator Mod

In a bizarre turn of events, the Ever Given ship, which recently made headlines for getting stuck in the Suez Canal and disrupting 12% of global trade, has now been immortalized in a Microsoft Flight Simulator mod. The mod, created by FlyBoyRez1 and inspired by Zepingouin35, recreates the Suez Canal traffic jam and features the Ever Given, surrounding traffic, parked boats in the Suez Gulf, and attempts by tugboats to free the ship.

The mod also includes a pair of excavators, recreating the popular digger meme, which has become a cultural nod to the event. The mod is available for download at Flight Sim.io, with additional required mods mentioned. YouTube content creator Bill4LE attempted to land a plane on the modded ship but failed, further showcasing the mod's attention to detail and realism.

Reddit users have also noted the Ever Given's presence in other video games like Rust and Valheim, highlighting the ship's newfound status as a cultural icon. The mod has become a humorous and educational simulation of the real-life event, allowing players to experience the challenges of navigating the Suez Canal and the efforts to free the stuck ship.
