Royal Anniversary: Prince Harry and Meghan Markle Privately Congratulate William and Kate

In a heartwarming display of family unity, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle privately congratulated Prince William and Kate Middleton on their tenth wedding anniversary. The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge celebrated their milestone anniversary on April 29th, marking a decade since their grand wedding at Westminster Abbey in 2011.

The royal family took to social media to share tributes and reminisce about the couple's special day. Queen Elizabeth's official Royal Family Instagram account and Prince Charles' Clarence House account posted anniversary tributes, featuring photos and videos from the wedding day. Prince Harry, who does not have a social media account, chose a private manner to send his congratulations.

The anniversary celebrations highlight the continued public interest and affection for the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge. Garrison Sergeant Major Bill Mott, who was involved in organizing the wedding procession, shared his memories of the day, revealing that he had to miss the ceremony for work reasons. Despite this, Mott emphasized the positive atmosphere and public excitement on the wedding day. The royal family's enduring popularity is a testament to their commitment to public service and their ability to connect with the British people.
