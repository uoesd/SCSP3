Pelosi Announces Plans for Independent Commission to Investigate Capitol Riot

In the aftermath of the Capitol riot on January 6, 2021, House Speaker Nancy Pelosi has announced plans to establish an independent commission to investigate the events surrounding the insurrection and the interference with the peaceful transfer of power. The commission aims to review the facts and causes related to the riot, which resulted in the deaths of five people, including a Capitol Police officer.

Pelosi made the announcement during a press conference, where she also mentioned supplemental spending to enhance security at the Capitol. "We must take a comprehensive look at the events that led up to the riot and ensure that something like this never happens again," Pelosi said.

The formation of the commission follows former President Donald Trump's acquittal in his second Senate impeachment trial. Trump was accused of "incitement of insurrection" for his role in the riot, but was acquitted with a bipartisan vote.
