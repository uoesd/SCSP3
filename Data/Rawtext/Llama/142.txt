Tucker Carlson's Exclusive Access to Kyle Rittenhouse Raises Questions About Media Influence and Vigilantism

In a surprising turn of events, Fox News host Tucker Carlson has gained exclusive access to Kyle Rittenhouse and his defense team during the trial that has captivated the nation.
