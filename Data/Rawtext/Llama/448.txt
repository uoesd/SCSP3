Prince Harry and Meghan Markle Named Among Time 100's Most Influential People

Sept 15 (Reuters) â€“ Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, the Duke and Duchess of Sussex, have been featured on the cover of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world issue, alongside other notable figures such as Billie Eilish, Britney Spears, and Simone Biles.

According to Time's editor in chief, Edward Felsenthal, the list highlights leaders working towards a better future. "These individuals are not just influential, they are also passionate about making a positive impact in the world," Felsenthal said in a statement.

Harry and Meghan, who left their royal duties and Britain in 2020, have been recognized for their active compassion and refusal to remain silent on important issues. Chef Jose Andres, founder of the nonprofit World Kitchen, penned a tribute to the couple, praising their commitment to using their platform to make a difference.

The list, which includes 54 women, also features political figures such as President Joe Biden, Vice President Kamala Harris, and former President Donald Trump.

Britney Spears, who has been a prominent figure in the entertainment industry for over two decades, was recognized for her fight against her conservatorship and her resilience. Reality star Paris Hilton, in a tribute to Spears, highlighted her joy and spirit amidst personal struggles.

Rapper Megan Thee Stallion praised Billie Eilish for her uniqueness, self-advocacy, and support for women. "Billie is a true original, and her music is a reflection of her bold and unapologetic spirit," Megan Thee Stallion said.

The Time 100 list has been published annually since 1999 and recognizes individuals who have made a significant impact in various fields, including politics, entertainment, sports, and technology.

In addition to Harry and Meghan, other notable figures featured on the list include climate activist Greta Thunberg, Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos, and tennis star Naomi Osaka.

The issue hits newsstands on September 17, and the full list can be found on Time's website.

In conclusion, the recognition of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, as well as other influential figures, highlights the importance of leadership and activism in shaping a better future for all.
