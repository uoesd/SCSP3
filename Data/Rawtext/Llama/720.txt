Threat of School Shooting Leads to Arrest and School Closure in Bristol Borough

– Bristol Borough, PA – A student from Bristol Borough High School was arrested on Monday for making a shooting threat against the school, leading to the closure of all Bristol Borough School District facilities for the day.

According to Sgt. Joe Moors, acting leader of the borough police, the threat was reported through the Safe2Say tip system, which mentioned a plan to shoot up the high school. The student, whose name has not been released, faces charges of making terroristic threats and disorderly conduct in juvenile court.

"We take all threats seriously, and we will not tolerate any behavior that puts the safety of our students and staff at risk," said Dr. Thomas F. Shaffer, acting superintendent of the Bristol Borough School District, in a letter to parents.

The arrest was made with the help of the Bucks County District Attorney's Office and Bucks County Juvenile Probation Department. No weapons were found with the student.

The threat comes on the heels of a recent shooting in Oxford, Michigan, which resulted in four student deaths. "We understand that this incident may cause concern for our community, but we want to assure everyone that we are taking all necessary precautions to ensure the safety of our students and staff," said Sgt. Moors.

The school district has announced that all schools will reopen on Tuesday with an increased police presence. "We will continue to work closely with law enforcement to ensure the safety and well-being of our students and staff," said Dr. Shaffer.

The incident is a reminder of the importance of reporting any suspicious activity or threats to the authorities. "We encourage anyone who hears or sees something to say something," said Sgt. Moors. "Together, we can keep our community safe."

The investigation is ongoing, and anyone with information is asked to contact the Bristol Borough Police Department.

In conclusion, the arrest of a student for making a shooting threat against Bristol Borough High School serves as a reminder of the importance of school safety and the need for vigilance in reporting suspicious activity. The school district and law enforcement are working together to ensure the safety of all students and staff, and the community is encouraged to remain vigilant and report any concerns to the authorities.
