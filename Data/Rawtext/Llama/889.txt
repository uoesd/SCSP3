Brady's Super Bowl Legacy: A Look at His 200-Plus Teammates

This number is set to grow as he prepares to take the field for his 10th Super Bowl.

Brady's top targets in the Super Bowl are Julian Edelman and Deion Branch, each with 24 catches. Edelman has been a crucial part of the Patriots' passing game, while Branch was a key factor in their Super Bowl XXXIX victory.

Jason Pierre-Paul, who joined the Patriots in 2020, has the unique opportunity to join an exclusive list of players who have both beaten and won a Super Bowl with Brady. Logan Mankins and Wes Welker, on the other hand, went to the Super Bowl multiple times with Brady but never won.

The name 'Brandon' is the most common among Brady's Super Bowl teammates, with nine individuals sharing the name. This includes Brandon Browner, Brandon Spikes, and Brandon Bolden, among others.

As Brady prepares to take the field for his 10th Super Bowl, his legacy as one of the greatest quarterbacks in NFL history is already cemented. His impressive collection of teammates, many of whom have gone on to achieve greatness in their own right, is a testament to his ability to elevate those around him and lead his team to victory.
