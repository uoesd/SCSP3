Prince Harry and Meghan Welcome Daughter Lilibet Diana, Paying Tribute to Queen Elizabeth and Princess Diana

In a heartwarming tribute to Queen Elizabeth and Princess Diana, Prince Harry and Meghan have named their daughter Lilibet Diana Mountbatten-Windsor. The little one arrived in Santa Barbara, California, weighing 7 pounds, 11 ounces, and both mother and baby are doing well.

The name Lilibet is a touching nod to Queen Elizabeth's childhood nickname, while Diana honors Harry's late mother. The couple's decision to name their daughter after these two iconic women is a testament to the significance of family and tradition.

In lieu of gifts, Harry and Meghan have requested that well-wishers support charities dedicated to improving the lives of women and girls. This gesture aligns with the couple's commitment to philanthropy and their desire to make a positive impact on the world.

Both Harry and Meghan will take parental leave from their work at the Archewell Foundation, which they established after stepping down as working members of the royal family in 2020. This decision allows them to focus on their growing family and continue their mission to drive positive change.

Lilibet is the Sussexes' second child and is eighth in line to the British throne. Her arrival comes after Meghan suffered a miscarriage in 2020, which she bravely shared in a New York Times op-ed. The couple's journey to expand their family has been marked by both joy and challenges, but their love and dedication to one another and their children is unwavering.

Harry and Meghan's decision to step down from their royal duties was a controversial one, and they have since aired grievances in a high-profile interview with Oprah. However, their commitment to their family and their philanthropic work remains unchanged.

Lilibet and her older brother Archie are the only biracial members of the British royal family, and their presence brings a beautiful diversity to the institution. As the royal family continues to evolve, Harry and Meghan's children will undoubtedly play a significant role in shaping its future.

In a statement, the couple expressed their gratitude to the public for their support and kindness. They are thrilled to welcome Lilibet to their family and are excited to watch her grow and thrive.
