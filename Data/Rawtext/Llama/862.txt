Tiger Woods Injured in Serious Car Accident After Spending Time with Dwyane Wade

Golf legend Tiger Woods was involved in a single-vehicle accident in Los Angeles County on Tuesday morning, resulting in hospitalization and surgery for multiple leg injuries. The accident occurred shortly after 7 a.m. local time and caused "major damage" to the vehicle, according to the Los Angeles Sheriff's Department.

Woods, who was in California for the Genesis Invitational, had spent time with former NBA player Dwyane Wade on Monday at a golf course. Wade shared their outing on Instagram, showing the two sports legends enjoying a round of golf together.

According to Mark Steinberg, Woods' agent, the golfer sustained multiple leg injuries in the crash and was undergoing surgery. Steinberg confirmed the accident and Woods' condition in a statement to the press.

Actor David Spade was also with Woods on Monday for a golf project. Spade took to social media to express his concern for Woods' well-being, writing, "I'm so glad I got to spend time with my buddy Tiger Woods yesterday. Thinking of him and wishing him a speedy recovery after his accident today."

The PGA Tour released a statement acknowledging the accident and offering support to Woods. "We are aware of the accident involving Tiger Woods and are offering our support and thoughts to him and his family," the statement read.

The Los Angeles Fire Department confirmed that Woods was the sole occupant of the vehicle and that he was transported to a local hospital for treatment. A spokesperson for the department told PEOPLE, "We responded to a call for a single-vehicle accident in the area of Hawthorne Boulevard and Blackhorse Road. The victim, a male adult, was transported to a local hospital for treatment."

Woods, who has been recovering from back surgery, was serving as host of the Genesis Invitational at the time of the accident. The tournament, which benefits the Tiger Woods Foundation, was held at the Riviera Country Club in Pacific Palisades, California.

The accident is currently under investigation by the Los Angeles Sheriff's Department. No further details have been released at this time.
