The shooting occurred on November 30, 2020, at Oxford High School, located about 30 miles north of Detroit.

The prosecution's case against the Crumbleys centers on their alleged failure to address their son's mental health issues and their decision to buy him a gun despite knowing his fascination with firearms.

"The Crumbleys had a responsibility to protect their son and the community from harm," Williams said. "They failed in that responsibility, and now four innocent lives have been lost."
