Tiger Woods Expresses Gratitude for Support Following Serious Car Accident

In a heartwarming display of solidarity, fellow golfers Rory Mcllroy, Justin Thomas, and others paid tribute to Tiger Woods during the WGC-Workday Championship by wearing his signature red shirt and black pants. Woods, who was involved in a serious car accident in Rancho Palos Verdes, California, was touched by the gesture, stating it helps him through this difficult time.

Collin Morikawa, who won the WGC-Workday Championship, acknowledged Woods in his victory speech, expressing his admiration for the golf legend and wishing him a speedy recovery.

Woods suffered a single-vehicle accident when his car hit a sign, sheared through a tree, and flipped over. He had to be extricated from the vehicle by first responders due to major damage, and underwent emergency surgery for significant orthopedic injuries to his right lower extremity. Following the initial surgery, Woods had follow-up procedures at Cedars-Sinai.

Dr. Anish Mahajan, Chief Medical Officer at Harbor-UCLA Medical Center, stated, "Mr. Woods suffered significant orthopedic injuries to his right lower extremity. He underwent emergency surgery to stabilize his injuries, and he is currently recovering at Cedars-Sinai."
