"Kyle told me that he was worried about being hurt or killed at the protests," Black said. "He said he wanted to protect himself and his friends."

The prosecution, however, has argued that Rittenhouse's actions were reckless and unnecessary, and that he should be held accountable for the deaths and injuries he caused.
