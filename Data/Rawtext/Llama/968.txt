However, Trump tested the ban evasion policy by tweeting from the @POTUS account, but those tweets were quickly removed. The Trump campaign's Twitter account was also permanently banned after attempting to share Trump's banned tweet.

In a statement, Twitter emphasized that their policy applies to all users, regardless of their political affiliation or position of power. They also differentiated between journalists reporting on Trump's words and accounts attempting to circumvent the ban by repeating those words.
