The official added that Harris will emphasize the need for a coordinated international response to future pandemics, building on the lessons learned from the COVID-19 pandemic. "The Vice President will make it clear that the United States is committed to working with our international partners to prevent the spread of infectious diseases and protect the health and well-being of people around the world," the official said.
