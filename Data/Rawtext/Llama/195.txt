In recent years, there have been several high-profile cyber attacks on pipelines, including a 2017 attack on the Wolf Creek nuclear power plant in Kansas.

These actions may include implementing stronger password policies, segmenting networks to limit the spread of malware, and increasing employee training to recognize and respond to cyber threats.

The TSA's expanded pipeline security staff will play a critical role in ensuring compliance with the new regulations. The agency has already been working closely with pipeline companies to identify and address vulnerabilities, and the additional staff will allow for more thorough and frequent assessments.
