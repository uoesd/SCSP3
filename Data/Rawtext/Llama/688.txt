Four Dead, Six Wounded in Michigan High School Shooting; Suspect Charged as Adult

A shooting at Oxford High School in Michigan has left four students dead and six others, including a teacher, wounded. The suspect, 15-year-old Ethan Crumbley, is in custody and has been charged as an adult with multiple counts, including murder and attempted murder.
