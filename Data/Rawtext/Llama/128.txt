Andrew Cuomo resigned from his position as Governor following multiple allegations of sexual misconduct. The Daily Show's video concludes with a mockery of Cuomo's response to the scandal, depicting him as a self-righteous news personality who is more concerned with his image than actual journalism. The clip has sparked a heated debate on social media, with some defending Cuomo and others praising The Daily Show for its bold take on the controversial issue.
