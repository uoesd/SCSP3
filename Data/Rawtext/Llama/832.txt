Tiger Woods' Car Crash Investigation Concludes, Cause Determined but Not Publicly Disclosed

The investigation into Tiger Woods' car crash in California has concluded, but the cause of the crash will not be publicly disclosed due to privacy issues requested by Woods' team, according to Los Angeles County Sheriff Alex Villanueva.

"We have completed our investigation and determined the cause of the crash, but we cannot release that information due to privacy concerns," Villanueva said in a statement. "We have reached out to Tiger Woods and his personnel to see if they would be willing to waive their privacy and allow us to release the information, but we have not received a response yet."
