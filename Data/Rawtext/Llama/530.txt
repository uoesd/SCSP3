Newark Schools Open Despite Tropical Storm Ida's Devastating Damage

Tropical Storm Ida wreaked havoc on New Jersey, causing at least 27 deaths and significant damage to infrastructure, including Newark's school buildings. Despite the catastrophic damage, Newark schools opened as scheduled, thanks to the tireless efforts of maintenance crews, custodians, and other employees.
