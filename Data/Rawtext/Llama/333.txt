In the latest development in the ongoing saga of QAnon conspiracy theories, followers of the movement have now turned their attention to the Ever Given ship, which has been stuck in the Suez Canal for several days. They believe that the current situation will unveil the alleged trafficking live on camera to the world.

QAnon's beliefs center around a global cabal of pedophiles allegedly involving leading Democrats and Hollywood elites. The conspiracy theory started with cryptic messages on message boards in 2017 by a figure known as "Q." Although no new "Qdrops" or messages have appeared for months, followers continue to reinterpret existing messages.

This is not the first time QAnon has made unfounded claims involving an Evergreen vessel. In August 2020, after the Beirut port explosion, QAnon followers claimed that an Evergreen ship was involved in the explosion, which was later debunked. These unfounded claims reflect QAnon's pattern of twisting world events to fit their conspiracy theories.
