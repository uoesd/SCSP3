The enhanced child tax credit will benefit single parents making up to $75,000 and joint filers making up to $150,000 a year. Additionally, the child tax credit has been made fully refundable, potentially benefiting 20 million more children. This means that even if a family's tax liability is zero, they will still receive the full amount of the credit.

The child tax credit payments are part of President Biden's American Rescue Plan, which aims to provide relief to families who have been struggling financially during the pandemic. The expanded credit is expected to lift 5 million children out of poverty and provide relief to millions more families who are struggling to make ends meet.
