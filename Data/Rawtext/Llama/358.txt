AOC Slams Facebook's Monopoly During Blackout, Highlighting Threat to Democracy

In the wake of Facebook's recent blackout, Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez (AOC) took the opportunity to criticize the tech giant's monopolistic behavior, arguing that its unchecked power poses a threat to democracy and free society.

During the blackout, which affected users worldwide, AOC took to Twitter to highlight Facebook's history of acquiring or destroying competitors, such as WhatsApp and Instagram. She emphasized that Facebook's dominance in the market allows it to stifle competition and limit choices for consumers.

"Facebook's monopolistic behavior is a threat to our democracy and free society," AOC tweeted. "We need to take a closer look at their business practices and ensure that they are not using their power to suppress competition and limit choices for consumers."
