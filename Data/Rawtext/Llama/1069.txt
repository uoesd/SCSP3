Various factors, including racial biases and a lack of newsroom diversity, contribute to this syndrome. However, social media and public engagement can challenge these biases by amplifying neglected cases.

While recent coverage of Petito's case might indicate a shift toward more inclusive reporting on missing persons, experts argue that without lasting changes in newsroom practices, the pattern of biased coverage is likely to recur.
