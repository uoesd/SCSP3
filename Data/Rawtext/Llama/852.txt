Tiger Woods' Crash Car, Genesis GV80, Earns Top Safety Pick+ Rating

The Genesis GV80 SUV, which was involved in Tiger Woods' frightening car crash in February, has been named a Top Safety Pick+ by the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS). This prestigious rating is a testament to the vehicle's exceptional crashworthiness and crash prevention capabilities.

According to Russ Rader, spokesman for IIHS, the GV80 scored high marks on the organization's rigorous assessments. "The Genesis GV80 performed well in our crash tests, and its advanced safety features helped it earn a Top Safety Pick+ rating," Rader said.

Brian Latouf, Chief Safety Officer at Genesis Motor North America, echoed Rader's sentiments. "We are thrilled to see the GV80 recognized for its outstanding safety performance," Latouf said. "Safety is a top priority for our brand, and we are committed to engineering vehicles that protect our customers on the road."

The GV80's impressive safety features include 10 standard airbags and forward-collision alert avoidance. These advanced technologies work together to minimize the risk of accidents and protect occupants in the event of a crash.

The IIHS's Top Safety Pick+ rating is reserved for vehicles that excel in both crashworthiness and crash prevention. The GV80's achievement is particularly noteworthy, given that it had not yet undergone independent safety assessments at the time of Woods' crash.

The importance of vehicle safety cannot be overstated. In 2019, over 36,000 people were killed in car crashes in the United States alone. The GV80's Top Safety Pick+ rating serves as a reminder of the life-saving potential of advanced safety features.

Some have speculated that the GV80's safety features may have contributed to saving Tiger Woods' life. While the exact circumstances of the crash are still under investigation, it is clear that the vehicle's robust safety features played a significant role in protecting its occupants.

The GV80 was not initially included in IIHS's Top Safety Pick+ awards due to its newness. However, Genesis' commitment to safety is evident in the vehicle's engineering and design. The GV80 starts at nearly $50,000, reflecting the brand's emphasis on safety and luxury.

In conclusion, the Genesis GV80's Top Safety Pick+ rating is a testament to the vehicle's exceptional safety performance. The car's advanced safety features and robust crashworthiness make it an excellent choice for those seeking a safe and reliable vehicle.
