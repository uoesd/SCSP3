Search for Brian Laundrie Continues as Arrest Warrant Issued in Connection to Gabby Petito's Death

NORTH PORT, Florida (WABC) – The search for Brian Laundrie continues into its sixth day, with authorities focused on the Carlton Reserve area he told his parents he was visiting. Despite the issuance of an arrest warrant related to Gabby Petito's death, the strategy for finding Laundrie remains unchanged, according to Commander Joseph Fussell, who is leading the search.

Laundrie has been indicted for unauthorized use of a Capital One debit card and pin between August 30 and September 1, with intent to defraud. The warrant was issued following an investigation into Laundrie's alleged use of Petito's bank card.

Gabby Petito's funeral is scheduled for Sunday at Moloney's Holbrook Funeral Home, open to the public and media. Petito was reported missing on September 11, and her body was later found in Grand Teton National Park, her death ruled a homicide.

The City of Moab, Utah, is investigating the police handling of a domestic incident between Petito and Laundrie on August 12. Witness Nina Celie Angelo has come forward, reporting an explosive argument between the couple at a Wyoming restaurant on August 27.

Divers have joined the search for Laundrie in Florida, focusing on bodies of water in Carlton Reserve, though no findings have been reported yet.

Moab Police have released a witness statement describing a domestic dispute between Petito and Laundrie in August. Chief Bret Edge, Moab City Police Chief, stated, "We are still actively investigating the circumstances surrounding Ms. Petito's death, and we urge anyone with information to come forward."

Commander Joseph Fussell emphasized the importance of finding Laundrie, saying, "We are committed to bringing Brian Laundrie to justice and providing closure for the Petito family."

The search for Laundrie continues, with authorities following up on all leads and tips. The public is urged to remain vigilant and report any sightings of Laundrie to the authorities immediately.

As the investigation unfolds, the nation remains captivated by the tragic story of Gabby Petito and the ongoing search for her missing fiancé, Brian Laundrie.

This is a developing story, and updates will be provided as new information becomes available.
