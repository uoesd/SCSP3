Underreporting and Misdirection

"This resulted in a gross undercount of the true toll of the pandemic on our most vulnerable population."

The report alleges that the Cuomo administration directed nursing homes to accept COVID-19 patients, a decision that critics say contributed to the high death rates among elderly residents.
