The discovery of the video comes as the FBI has likely found Petito's body in a Wyoming national park. Teton County Coroner Dr. Brent Blue confirmed the discovery of a body at Bridger-Teton National Forest. "The cause of death is still under investigation," Dr. Blue said.

The discovery of the body was deemed "heartbreaking" by Laundrie family's attorney. "The Laundrie family is still mourning the loss of their daughter, Cassie, and are now faced with another unimaginable tragedy," the attorney said.

Petito went missing last month during a trip with Laundrie, who returned home without her and then disappeared. Laundrie is currently the subject of a nationwide manhunt.
