H-E-B Adjusts Operations Amid Winter Storm Uri's Devastating Impact

San Antonio, Texas - In response to the unprecedented winter storm that has gripped Texas, H-E-B has temporarily closed some stores and reduced operating hours in affected areas to prioritize the safety of customers and employees.

The storm, known as Winter Storm Uri, has brought record-breaking cold temperatures, leading to widespread power outages and water shortages across the state. Millions of Texans are without essential services, and the food supply chain has been severely disrupted.

To conserve energy and support the Texas energy grid, H-E-B has implemented temporary store closures and reduced operating hours in the following regions:

  • Greater Houston
  • North East region
  • Gulf Coast
  • Austin
  • Central Texas
  • San Antonio

Store Hours and Closures

Most affected stores are opening at 9 or 10 a.m. and closing around 5 to 7 p.m. Pharmacy hours align with store hours. However, due to extreme weather conditions, stores in Austin, Central Texas, and San Antonio have been closed until further notice.

Customers are advised to check the H-E-B website or call their local store for the most up-to-date information on store hours and closures.

Supply Chain Disruptions

The winter storm has caused significant supply chain disruptions, leading to limited product assortments and purchase limits at some locations. H-E-B is working diligently to replenish inventory as quickly as possible.

Online Grocery Services Suspended

Due to the severe weather conditions, H-E-B has temporarily suspended online grocery services, including curbside and home delivery. The company is prioritizing in-store shopping to ensure that customers have access to essential items.

Unprecedented Event

H-E-B describes Winter Storm Uri as an unprecedented event that has caused severe disruptions to the food supply chain. The company is committed to providing essential services to the communities it serves and is working around the clock to address the challenges posed by the storm.

Safety First

The safety of customers and employees remains H-E-B's top priority. The company urges customers to exercise caution when traveling to stores and to follow all safety guidelines.

Store Hours and Operations Subject to Change

Store hours and operations are subject to change based on local conditions. Customers are encouraged to check the H-E-B website or call their local store for the latest information.

Community Support

H-E-B is actively supporting the community during this challenging time. The company has donated food and supplies to local food banks and is working with community partners to provide assistance to those in need.

Additional Information

For the latest updates on H-E-B's response to Winter Storm Uri, customers can visit the company's website at heb.com or follow H-E-B on social media.
