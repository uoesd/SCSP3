Tragedy on 'Rust' Set: Cinematographer Killed, Director Injured in Prop Gun Misfire

A tragic incident has occurred on the set of the Western film "Rust," resulting in the death of cinematographer Halyna Hutchins and the injury of director Joel Souza. The incident, which involved a prop gun misfiring, has sent shockwaves through the entertainment industry.

According to the Santa Fe Sheriff's Department, the shooting took place around 1:50 p.m. local time at the Bonanza Creek Ranch set in New Mexico. Alec Baldwin, who stars in and is producing the film, was reportedly holding the prop gun when it discharged, striking Hutchins and Souza.

Hutchins was airlifted to the University of New Mexico Hospital, where she succumbed to her injuries. Souza was transported to Christus St. Vincent Regional Medical Center, where he is receiving treatment for his injuries.

A photo taken shortly after the incident shows Baldwin visibly distraught, being comforted by a crew member. The Santa Fe Sheriff's Department is currently investigating the shooting, and no charges have been filed at this time.

Rust Movie Productions LLC, the production company behind the film, has released a statement expressing their devastation over the tragedy. "We are deeply saddened by the tragic accident that occurred today on the set of our film, Rust," the statement reads. "Our thoughts are with the family and loved ones of Halyna Hutchins, and we are fully cooperating with the Santa Fe Sheriff's Department's investigation."

The production company has also announced that filming has been paused indefinitely. "The safety of our cast and crew is our highest priority, and we are taking this pause to allow for the investigation to be completed," the statement continues.

Counseling services will be provided to those connected to the film, including cast, crew, and production staff.

Tributes have poured in for Hutchins, who was known for her talent and dedication to her craft. Colleagues have praised her as a rising star in the cinematography field, with a bright future ahead of her.

The incident has raised questions about the safety protocols surrounding the use of prop guns on film sets. The entertainment industry has a long history of accidents involving firearms, and this latest tragedy has reignited calls for stricter safety measures.

The investigation into the shooting is ongoing, and more details are expected to emerge in the coming days. The entertainment industry and the public alike are mourning the loss of Halyna Hutchins and sending their thoughts and prayers to her family and loved ones.
