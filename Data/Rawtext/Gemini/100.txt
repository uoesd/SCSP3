Aquila's Nest Vineyards Celebrates One Year with Spooktacular Scavenger Hunt and More

Newtown, CT (October 15, 2021) - Aquila's Nest Vineyards, a beloved winery nestled in the heart of Newtown, is thrilled to commemorate its first year of operation with a series of captivating events, including the upcoming "Halo-Wine Scavenger Hunt."

Halo-Wine Scavenger Hunt: A Spooky Adventure

On October 30th, Aquila's Pumpkin Yard will transform into a thrilling outdoor playground for the "Halo-Wine Scavenger Hunt." This unique event invites participants to embark on a captivating journey through the vineyard, following clues that delve into the secrets of winemaking and Aquila's Nest Vineyards.

"We're excited to host this fun and educational event that promotes social distancing entertainment," said Neviana Zhgaba, Co-founder of Aquila's Nest Vineyards. "Participants will have a blast while learning about our wines and the vineyard."

To participate in the scavenger hunt, reservations are required, and a $3 entry fee per person is applicable. Upon successful completion of the hunt, players will be rewarded with a complimentary glass of wine, provided they are 21 years of age or older.

A Year of Unforgettable Experiences

Aquila's Nest Vineyards has quickly become a cherished destination in the Greater Danbury area, offering a wide range of activities for locals and visitors alike. The vineyard's picturesque setting, coupled with its family-friendly atmosphere, has garnered rave reviews.

"My family and I had an amazing time at Aquila's Nest," said a satisfied patron. "The atmosphere was so welcoming, and the wines were delicious. We can't wait to go back."

Upcoming Events: A Calendar of Delight

In addition to the "Halo-Wine Scavenger Hunt," Aquila's Nest Vineyards has a plethora of upcoming events designed to provide entertainment during these times of social distancing. These events include:

  • Chocolate & Wine Pairing: Indulge in a delectable pairing of fine wines and artisanal chocolates.
  • Hallowine Sunset Art Walk: Stroll through the vineyard at sunset, admiring local art while sipping on exquisite wines.

Most events feature live music and food trucks, ensuring a lively and enjoyable experience for all attendees.

Danbury: A City that Shines

Aquila's Nest Vineyards is proud to be a part of the vibrant community of Danbury, CT. Known for its bustling nightlife and cultural attractions, Danbury is a city that truly glows at night.

"We're thrilled to be part of such a thriving community," said Zhgaba. "Danbury is a wonderful place to live, work, and play."

About Aquila's Nest Vineyards

Aquila's Nest Vineyards is a family-owned and operated winery located in the picturesque town of Newtown, CT. The vineyard produces a variety of award-winning wines, including Chardonnay, Cabernet Franc, and Merlot. Aquila's Nest Vineyards is committed to providing a welcoming and enjoyable experience for all visitors.

For more information about Aquila's Nest Vineyards and its upcoming events, please visit their website at www.aquilasnestvineyards.com.
