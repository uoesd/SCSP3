Oxford, Michigan - The Oxford community is reeling in the aftermath of a horrific shooting at Oxford High School that claimed the lives of four students and injured eight others, some critically. The Victims

The victims of the shooting have been identified as 16-year-old Tate Myre, 14-year-old Hana St. Juliana, 17-year-old Madisyn Baldwin, and 17-year-old Justin Shilling. The Suspect

The suspect, a 15-year-old sophomore at Oxford High School, was arrested shortly after the shooting. The firearm used in the shooting was recently purchased by the suspect's father.

The Investigation
