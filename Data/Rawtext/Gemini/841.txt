Tiger Woods' Golf Lessons with Celebrities Released After Devastating Car Crash

In a poignant twist of fate, Tiger Woods' miniseries, "A Round with Tiger: Celebrity Lessons," was filmed just a day before his life-altering car crash in California. The series, which premiered with Woods' blessing, offers a glimpse into the golf legend's life and the challenges he faces as he navigates the twilight of his career.

A Day Before Tragedy

On February 22, 2021, Woods filmed the premiere episode of the series with actress Jada Pinkett Smith at Rolling Hills Country Club in Los Angeles. The episode, which aired on Golf Digest on July 6, 2021, features Woods discussing the physical toll of aging and the impact it has had on his body.

"I'm not getting any younger," Woods said in the episode. "My body is starting to break down a little bit. I'm not able to do the things I used to do."

Woods' Physical Struggles

Woods' words have taken on a new resonance in light of his recent accident. The 45-year-old golfer has undergone 11 major surgeries, including an emergency surgery for the injuries he sustained in the February 23 crash.

"The recovery has been more painful than anything I have ever experienced," Woods said in a statement released after the accident.

Celebrity Support

Woods' fellow celebrities have expressed their shock and support following the accident. Dwyane Wade, who appeared in the second episode of "A Round with Tiger," said he was "devastated" by the news.

"I'm just praying for him and his family," Wade said. "I know he's a fighter, and I know he'll come back from this."

Cause of Accident

The Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department determined that speed was the primary cause of Woods' accident. There were no signs of impairment, and Woods was not cited.

Woods' Recovery

Woods is currently focused on his recovery and has expressed his gratitude for the support he has received during this difficult time.

"I'm so thankful for the outpouring of support and prayers I've received from the golf community and beyond," Woods said. "Your messages have meant the world to me."

Celebrity Lessons

"A Round with Tiger: Celebrity Playing Lessons" airs Tuesdays on Golf Digest through August 10. The series features Woods teaching golf to a variety of celebrities, including David Spade, Justin Thomas, and Annika Sorenstam.

Woods' Legacy

Despite the setbacks he has faced, Woods remains one of the most iconic figures in sports history. His determination and resilience are an inspiration to millions around the world.

As he recovers from his injuries, Woods' legacy will continue to grow. His impact on the game of golf is undeniable, and his story is a testament to the human spirit's ability to overcome adversity.
