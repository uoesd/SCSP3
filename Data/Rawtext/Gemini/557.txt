"We will not give up our country to the Taliban," said Ahmad Massoud, the son of former Afghan resistance leader Ahmad Shah Massoud. "We will fight for our freedom."

The Taliban have condemned the resistance, calling it a "conspiracy" against the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan.

The situation in Afghanistan remains fluid and unpredictable. The Taliban have established control over most of the country, but resistance is growing in some areas.
